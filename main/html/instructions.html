How to Play?

Instructions:

  • There are 5 levels
  • Complete each level by finding the missing digit within 30 seconds.
  • Score 50 points to move on to next level and forth
  • If you click an incorrect digit, you will lose a life. Total number of lives are set to 4.
  • If you couldnt answer the question within 30 seconds, you will lose a life
  • Click the correct digits and beat all levels.
